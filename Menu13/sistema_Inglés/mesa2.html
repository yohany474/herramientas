    Historia

    Once upon a time, there was a young girl named Emily who had a big dream of becoming a famous singer. She knew that achieving her dream would require a lot of hard work and dedication, but she was determined to make it happen. One day, Emily decided to take action and make a plan for achieving her goal. She sat down and wrote out a list of all the things she needed to do, from practicing her singing every day to networking with industry professionals. As she looked at her list, she realized that some of the items would require her to make some major changes in her life. She would need to start taking voice lessons, save up money to record a demo, and even move to a bigger city where there were more opportunities for aspiring singers. But Emily was confident that she could do it, and she knew that with hard work and determination, she would achieve her dream. She decided to use both "will" and "going to" to talk about her future plans. She said to herself, "I will work hard every day to improve my singing skills, and I am going to save up enough money to record a demo. I will also start looking for a vocal coach, and I am going to move to a bigger city where I can meet more people in the music industry." And so, Emily set out on her journey to become a famous singer. She worked hard every day, practiced her singing, and followed her plan. And in the end, her hard work paid off. She landed a recording contract and became a successful singer, all because she believed in herself and was willing to do whatever it took to achieve her dreams.
